Welcome to Formula 1

Formula 1 is a premier motorsport known for its speed, skill, and technology. Since its inception, it has captured the hearts of racing fans around the world.

The Golden Age of F1

The 1950s

Formula 1’s roots go back to the 1950s, an era of daring drivers and groundbreaking cars. This was the age of legends like Juan Manuel Fangio.

The 1960s

The 1960s brought technical advancements and new safety measures. Drivers like Jim Clark dominated the circuits with style and precision.

The Modern Era

The 2000s

In the 2000s, F1 became faster, safer, and more competitive. With Michael Schumacher’s dominance, Ferrari set a new benchmark in motorsport.

The 2010s

The 2010s saw the rise of hybrid technology and aerodynamics, with stars like Lewis Hamilton taking the sport to new heights.
